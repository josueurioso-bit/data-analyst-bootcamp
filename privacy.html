This policy follows the Five C's of Ethical Data Collection. We believe you deserve to know exactly what happens with your information.

What We Collect

When you take the Milestone 0 Assessment quiz, we collect your scores across six skill areas:

  • Numeracy (basic math)
  • Reading comprehension
  • Computer literacy
  • Logical thinking
  • Communication
  • Learning mindset

We also record the time you took the quiz and your overall readiness level (a 1-5 scale based on your scores).

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your specific answers to individual questions
  • Any personal identifying information
About IP Addresses: We store a "hashed" version of your IP address. This is a one-way scrambled code that helps us prevent system abuse but cannot be reversed to find your actual location. Think of it like a fingerprint that shows uniqueness but doesn't reveal identity.

Why We Collect It

We use this data to discover which foundational skills students struggle with most. For example, if we find that 68% of students score poorly on reading comprehension, we know to create better reading support materials.

This helps us:

  • Improve the bootcamp curriculum based on real student needs
  • Prioritize which foundational skills to teach first
  • Create targeted prep materials for common weak areas
  • Make data-driven decisions instead of guessing
How We Use Your Data: Your individual scores are combined with everyone else's to find patterns. We never look at one person's results in isolation or try to identify individual students.

Your Rights & Choices

You can choose not to share your data. Before you start the quiz, you'll see a consent checkbox. If you uncheck it:

  • You can still take the full assessment
  • You'll still see your personal results on screen
  • Your scores simply won't be saved to our database

There is no penalty for opting out. You get the exact same assessment experience either way.

Important Note: Because your data is anonymous (no name or email attached), we cannot identify which specific records belong to you if you later want them deleted. This is actually a privacy protection â€” even if our database were compromised, no one could link the data back to you.

How We Protect Your Data

  • No personal identifying information is stored
  • IP addresses are hashed using SHA-256 encryption (one-way, irreversible)
  • Database access is restricted to bootcamp administrators only
  • Data is used solely for curriculum improvement
  • We never sell or share individual data with third parties

The Five C's in Practice

Here's how we implement each principle:

  • Consent: You explicitly choose whether to share data via checkbox
  • Clarity: This policy is written at an 8th-grade reading level
  • Control: Opting out doesn't prevent you from using the quiz
  • Compensation: Your data helps improve a free educational resource
  • Consequence Awareness: We explain exactly what happens with your data

Questions?

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle data, please reach out to the bootcamp team.
